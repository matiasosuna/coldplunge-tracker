What cold does
to your body

Cold immersion is one of the most researched wellness tools available.

01

Faster muscle recovery

Cold constricts blood vessels and clears metabolic waste, cutting soreness so you can train harder and more often.

02

Mental clarity & mood

A single session triggers up to a 300% spike in norepinephrine — the neurotransmitter behind focus, drive, and mood.

03

Stress resilience

The cold is a training ground for your nervous system. Learn to stay calm under pressure — it carries over into life.

04

Metabolic activation

Cold activates brown adipose tissue, increasing caloric burn and improving insulin sensitivity over time.

05

Deeper sleep

Post-immersion temperature rebound signals the brain for rest. Most people report noticeably better sleep the same night.
