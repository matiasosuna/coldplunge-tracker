Why cold exposure

What happens when
you enter the ice

Cold immersion is one of the most researched wellness tools available. Here's what the science shows.

⚡

Faster Muscle Recovery

Cold constricts blood vessels and reduces metabolic waste build-up, cutting soreness and helping you train harder, more often.

🧠

Mental Clarity

A single immersion triggers up to a 300% spike in norepinephrine — the neurotransmitter behind focus, mood, and alertness.

🔥

Metabolic Activation

Cold activates brown adipose tissue, increasing caloric expenditure and improving insulin sensitivity over time.

🛡️

Immune Resilience

Regular cold exposure has been associated with higher white blood cell counts and reduced sick days in multiple studies.

🧘

Stress Hardening

The cold teaches controlled breathing and calm under pressure — a skill that transfers directly to everyday life.

😴

Deeper Sleep

Post-immersion temperature rebound signals the brain for sleep. Most people report noticeably better rest the same night.

The process

Your session,
step by step

01

Reserve your spot

Sessions are intentionally small. Book online in advance — this isn't the kind of experience you want to miss because it filled up.

02

Breathwork warm-up

We open with guided breathing to regulate your nervous system and set the right mindset before you touch the water.

03

Guided immersion

Enter the ice at your own pace with a facilitator present. No pressure. The only rule is you stay present.

04

Natural warm-up

No hot showers. We let the body do the work — and debrief as a group while the warmth comes back on its own.
